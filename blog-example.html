Spider-Man 2 Prowler Stashes - all locationsIf you played Spider-Man: Miles Morales, you know the titular hero's uncle Aaron has a complicated criminal history as Prowler, a burglarizing bad guy. But in , he's getting back on the straight and narrow, and he needs Miles to help him clean up his Prowler Stashes. That works out for Miles, since he gets to keep the XP and resources he earns for doing so. Here's where to find all Prowler Stashes.

Spider-Man 2 Prowler Stashes - all locations

There are eight Prowler Stashes hidden across the city, as well as a ninth final mission/stash (marked in red below) that only appears once you've completed the other eight. Each Prowler Stash involves an environmental puzzle that you'll need to solve, usually asking you to manipulate an area from specific vantage points using your webs. You'll find the stashes in the following districts and locations:

All Spider-Man 2 Prowler Stashes and locations

  • Astoria
  • Hell's Kitchen
  • Downtown Queens
  • Greenwich
  • Downtown Brooklyn
  • Midtown
  • Little Odessa
  • Williamsburg
